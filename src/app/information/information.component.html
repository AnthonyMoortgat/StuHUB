How to manage your components ?

Inscription module
How to use the inscription-module in a few steps.

Firstable you have to select the options of your module in the Inscription options.
There you will be able to select if you want an setting to be activated, required and or visible.
You can do that by turning the switch button on or off.

Than you would be able to preview the registred members and the selected settings in the last tab.
That's also what you will be able to see on your web page.

Next to the preview and the setting tab you can also directly register a new member by using the input tab (second tab).

Further you can also see all the registred members with all their details in the data tab. These informations will not be visible on your webpage.
